## 4. Italy Position on the Inglehart-Welzel Cultural Map

**Source:** https://www.worldvaluessurvey.org/WVSContents.jsp?CMSID=Findings

### Cultural Dimensions:
1. **Traditional vs Secular-rational values** (vertical axis)
2. **Survival vs Self-expression values** (horizontal axis)

### Italy's Position:
- **Quadrant:** Traditional + Self-expression (upper-left)
- **Characteristics:**
  - High emphasis on religion, parent-child ties, deference to authority
  - High levels of national pride and nationalistic outlook
  - Rejection of divorce, abortion, euthanasia and suicide
  - High self-expression values (environmental protection, tolerance, gender equality)
  - Growing demands for participation in decision-making

### Comparative Positioning:
- **Similar countries:** U.S., most of Latin America, Ireland
- **Opposite quadrant:** Sweden, Norway, Japan, Benelux, Germany, France (Secular-rational + Self-expression)
