**Italy — Total Fertility Rate (TFR): Data Summary**

**Historical trend (sources: ISTAT, World Bank, OECD, Eurostat)**

- 1960s: TFR approximately 2.5 — above replacement level
- 1970s: rapid decline begins
- 1995: TFR 1.19 — historical minimum of that period
- 2000s: partial recovery
- 2008: TFR 1.44 — peak of the recovery phase
- 2012 onward: renewed decline
- 2022: TFR 1.24
- 2023: TFR 1.20
- 2024: TFR approximately 1.20–1.30 depending on source

**European comparison 2024 (source: Eurostat)**

- France: 1.61
- Slovenia: 1.52
- EU average: 1.34
- Italy: below 1.30
- Spain: 1.10
- Malta: 1.01
- Italy groups with Estonia, Greece, Spain, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Poland, Finland as below-EU-average countries

**Mean age at first birth**

- EU average: 29.9 years
- Italy: 31.9 years — highest value in the entire EU
